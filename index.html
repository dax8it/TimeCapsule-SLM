TimeCapsule-SLM

Complete AI-powered research & creative platform with DeepResearch

Major Features:
🧠 In-Browser RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) with local vector store—privacy-first, no server required.
🔗 TimeCapsule Sharing: Export and load research sessions as .timecapsule.json files for seamless collaboration.
📚 Knowledge Base Integration: Upload your own documents, PDFs, and images to enhance research.
🤖 Local LLM Support: Ollama, LM Studio, OpenAI, and more.

Export your entire research structure and output as a .timecapsule.json file. Load a TimeCapsule to instantly restore topics, research output, and session metadata—enabling seamless sharing and collaboration.
